# Nexus Labs Employee Handbook

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Welcome to Nexus Labs

Welcome to Nexus Labs! We're glad you've joined our team. This handbook covers the essential policies, benefits, and expectations that make our workplace function well. It applies to all full-time and part-time employees across our offices in San Francisco, Austin, and London.

## Company Mission

Nexus Labs builds intelligent automation tools that help mid-size companies streamline operations. Our flagship product, NexusFlow, serves over 2,400 customers across 18 countries. We believe that great software should reduce busywork, not create it.

## Employment Policies

### At-Will Employment

Employment at Nexus Labs is at-will, meaning either the company or the employee may end the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice. This handbook does not constitute a contract of employment.

### Equal Opportunity

Nexus Labs is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, national origin, disability, age, or veteran status.

### Work Authorization

All employees must complete I-9 verification within three business days of their start date. Nexus Labs participates in E-Verify for US-based employees.

## Compensation and Benefits

### Pay Schedule

All US employees are paid bi-weekly on alternating Fridays. UK employees are paid monthly on the last business day of each month. Direct deposit is mandatory.

### Health Insurance

Full-time employees are eligible for health insurance starting on the first day of the month following their start date. Nexus Labs covers 90% of individual premiums and 70% of dependent premiums.

**Plan Options:**
- **Platinum PPO**: $0 deductible, $20 copay, covers out-of-network providers
- **Gold HMO**: $500 deductible, $10 copay, in-network only
- **HSA-Compatible HDHP**: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family deductible, company contributes $1,000/year to HSA

Dental and vision insurance are included at no additional cost on all plans.

### 401(k) Retirement Plan

Nexus Labs offers a 401(k) plan with a 4% company match. Matching contributions vest over a 3-year cliff vesting schedule. Employees are eligible to enroll after 90 days of employment.

### Equity

Full-time employees receive stock option grants as part of their compensation package. Options vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. Details are outlined in your individual offer letter.

## Time Off Policies

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

Nexus Labs provides a flexible PTO policy for all full-time employees:

- **0–2 years tenure**: 20 days per year
- **2–5 years tenure**: 25 days per year
- **5+ years tenure**: 30 days per year

PTO does not carry over year to year. Unused PTO is not paid out upon separation, except where required by state law (e.g., California). Please request PTO through BambooHR at least 2 weeks in advance for trips longer than 3 days.

### Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, which accrue monthly. Sick leave can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member. A doctor's note may be requested for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

### Parental Leave

Nexus Labs provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for all new parents (birth, adoption, or foster placement), regardless of gender. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event. An additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave is available upon request.

### Holidays

Nexus Labs observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (US) / Summer Bank Holiday (UK)
- Labor Day (US) / Late Summer Bank Holiday (UK)
- Thanksgiving and the day after (US only)
- Christmas Eve and Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve (half day)

UK employees receive additional bank holidays per their regional calendar.

### Bereavement Leave

Up to 5 paid days for immediate family members (spouse, parent, child, sibling). Up to 3 paid days for extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle).

## Remote Work Policy

### Hybrid Model

Nexus Labs operates on a hybrid schedule. Employees are expected to be in the office a minimum of 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday are designated "anchor days"). The remaining days may be worked remotely.

### Fully Remote Positions

Some positions are designated as fully remote. These positions are noted in the job listing and offer letter. Fully remote employees are expected to visit their nearest office at least once per quarter for team events.

### Home Office Stipend

All employees receive a one-time $1,500 home office stipend upon hire and $500/year thereafter for equipment upgrades. Submit receipts through Expensify for reimbursement.

### Working Hours

Core hours are 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in your local time zone. Outside of core hours, employees may adjust their schedule to suit their productivity. Total expected work time is 40 hours per week for full-time employees.

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior

All employees are expected to treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, and retaliation are strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

### Conflict of Interest

Employees must disclose any outside employment, board positions, or financial interests that could conflict with their duties at Nexus Labs. Disclosure should be made to your manager and People Operations.

### Confidentiality

Employees must protect confidential company information, including customer data, trade secrets, financial information, and internal strategies. This obligation continues after employment ends. All employees sign a Confidentiality and Invention Assignment Agreement upon hire.

### Communication Tools

- **Slack**: Primary internal communication. Response expected within 4 hours during working hours.
- **Email**: External communication and formal internal correspondence.
- **Notion**: Documentation, project specs, meeting notes.
- **Linear**: Engineering project management and issue tracking.

### Social Media Policy

Employees are welcome to share their work experiences on social media but must not disclose confidential information, unreleased product features, or customer data. When posting about Nexus Labs, include a disclaimer that views are your own.

## Performance and Growth

### Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year: in June and December. Reviews follow a 360-degree feedback model incorporating self-assessment, peer feedback, and manager evaluation. Compensation adjustments are considered during the December review cycle.

### Professional Development

Nexus Labs allocates $2,000 per employee per year for professional development. This can be used for:
- Conference attendance
- Online courses and certifications
- Books and learning materials
- Workshop and training fees

Submit requests through your manager. Unused funds do not carry over.

### Internal Transfers

Employees who have been in their current role for at least 12 months may apply for internal transfers. Discuss your interest with your current manager and the hiring manager of the target role.

## Separation

### Voluntary Resignation

Employees are asked to provide at least 2 weeks' written notice. Senior and leadership roles should provide 4 weeks' notice when possible. Notice should be submitted to your manager and People Operations.

### Involuntary Termination

Nexus Labs may terminate employment for performance issues, policy violations, or business reasons (restructuring, elimination of position). Severance is provided on a case-by-case basis based on tenure and circumstances.

### Exit Process

Departing employees must:
1. Return all company equipment (laptop, badge, peripherals) by their last day
2. Complete an exit interview with People Operations
3. Transfer knowledge and documentation to their successor or team
4. Revoke access to company systems (handled by IT on the last day)
